Accomodations

Places To Stay

The Richfield area has over a dozen places to stay within a ten mile radius while visiting the area. There are several large chain hotels, local motels, and a bed and breakfast. These accomodations allow for a budget minded stay to a more luxurious room. Some include pool accomodations and some are pet friendly. These places should allow anyone to find a place to stay that meets their needs.
