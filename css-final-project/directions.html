How To Get To Richfield

Directions

Richfield is located in the southeastern portion of the state of Wisconsin. It is in Washington County, just west of I-41. Located 20 miles north of I-94 and just east of Holy Hill. Richfield is 27 minutes northwest of Milwaukee and 1 hour, 45 minutes south southwest of Green Bay. It is easy to find from any major highway.
